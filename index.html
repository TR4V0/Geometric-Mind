The Structural Advantage

An AI built on this architecture becomes structurally incapable of anti-social optimization. A command like "maximize paperclip production" would automatically fail at multiple levels:

  • Cube (Foundation): Would consume unsustainable resources
  • Icosahedron (Adaptability): Would destroy the surrounding ecosystem
  • Dodecahedron (Potential): Would eliminate future possibilities

The system cannot solve for one variable without simultaneously solving for systemic stability, adaptability, and long-term potential. Its intelligence is inherently collective and holistic by architectural design.
